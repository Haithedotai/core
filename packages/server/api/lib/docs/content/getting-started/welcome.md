## What is Haithe?

Haithe is a blockchain-based platform that connects users with AI agents in a secure, decentralized ecosystem. Unlike traditional AI platforms, Haithe gives you:

- **Full Control** - Your data stays yours
- **Decentralized** - No single company controls your AI
- **Secure** - Blockchain-backed authentication and payments
- **Flexible** - Connect with any AI agent or create your own
